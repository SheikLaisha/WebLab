    DESCRIPTION

To facilitate students with easy self-learning, here at Science and Technology Club we provide a technical exposure to students by having them engaged in Conferences, Seminars and Workshops of National and International level. Students get thoroughly guided regarding research,research journals and in writing research papers. You will also be nurtured in a way that will help you build several projects and organize events. At the end of the day a college club is for your development and here at Science and Technology Club we value that aspect by mentoring and providing best guidance, resources while also introducing you to a community of like minded people looking forward to each other's progress and being there for each other.The Outcomes are Development of knowledge and skills , Ability to become interactive , Understanding of various languages , Practical and real approach towards problem solving.


    ABOUT US








The club organises various activities and workshops under experienced trainers. There is no limitation to only technical activities but also we organise intra and inter level competition. Keeping the utmost coordination with the competitive world, we believe in polishing soft skills, and enhancing competent personality. Everything seems incomplete if the rewards are not mentioned. So we appreciate and boost the confidence of participants and winners with a token of motivation, exciting rewards and trophies. We support and welcome new ideas related to discovery and innovation in the vast expanse of science and technology. The Objectives are it Provide an exposure to students through participation in National conferences, seminars and workshops and Provide information about research journals also Learning through fun activities and innovative projects.
The Outcomes are Development of knowledge and skills , Ability to become interactive , Understanding of various languages , Practical and real approach towards problem solving.
